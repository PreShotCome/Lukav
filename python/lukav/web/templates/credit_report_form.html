Credit-report ingest

Upload one of your 3-bureau reports (PDF) from annualcreditreport.com. Lukav pulls every negative tradeline — collections, charge-offs, sold debts — and lets you create a collection account for each in one shot.

You're encouraged to upload all three bureaus separately (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion) — duplicates across bureaus are useful: each tradeline can be disputed at every bureau it appears.

Heads up: extraction takes a few minutes. Lukav splits the report into chunks and asks Claude about each one — for a 48-page report that's typically 2–5 LLM calls of 30–60 seconds each. The page will look idle until it's done. Do not refresh; let it finish.
